WEBSITES SOLUTIONS (SOFTWARE FIRM)

Website Solutions is a Kenyan based software company that operates
globally to provide technology innovations. We are a client based software
company that is aimed at delivering quality and timely solutions to
clients. We offer development services ranging from Organization ERP,
Website Design and Development, E-Commerce systems, administrative
systems among others. We also offer Biometric Time attendance solution
to organizations and hotels and E-Learning Resources
development(Instructional Designs) for various organizations.

The company was established in 2018 as a business partnership between
three enthusiastic software developers who came together to provide a
range of ICT solutions to give businesses higher competitive edge in the
world market. The company later registered with the government of
Kenya so as to provide solutions beyond boundaries. Since then the
company has been improving its operational scales through consistent
innovations.
